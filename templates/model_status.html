Detailed Metrics Explanation

Classification Metrics

Accuracy ({{ status.accuracy }})

Overall correctness of the model across all predictions.

Precision ({{ status.precision }})

Of all positive predictions made, how many were correct.

Recall ({{ status.recall }})

Of all actual positive cases, how many did the model correctly identify.

F1 Score ({{ status.f1_score }})

Harmonic mean of precision and recall.

Performance Indicators

AUC-ROC ({{ status.auc_roc }})

Area Under the ROC curve. Measures class distinction ability.

Loss ({{ status.loss }})

Training loss. Lower values indicate better model fit.

Training Continuity
