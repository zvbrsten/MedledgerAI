About This Ledger

Every federated learning event is permanently recorded on a local Ethereum blockchain (Hardhat). Records cannot be modified or deleted — this is the tamper-proof audit trail.

What is logged: each hospital weight submission (with SHA-256 hash of weights), each FedAvg aggregation (with SHA-256 hash of global model), and each hospital login. No patient data ever appears here — only hashes and metrics.
